Crawler drive systems use tracks for enhanced maneuverability and grip on various surfaces, making them effective for navigating uneven pool floors and walls. In contrast, wheel drive systems utilize wheels for movement, offering speed and efficiency on flat surfaces. Consumers choose between these types based on pool shape, size, and specific cleaning needs, impacting overall performance and effectiveness in maintaining clean, debris-free pools.</p></p>
