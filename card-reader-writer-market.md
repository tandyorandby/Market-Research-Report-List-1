<p><p>The card reader writer market is divided into two main types: wireless and wired. Wireless card readers utilize Bluetooth or NFC technology, enabling transactions without physical connections, offering portability and convenience for mobile payments. They are ideal for businesses on the go. Wired card readers, on the other hand, connect directly to devices via USB or other ports, providing stable and secure connections. While less mobile, they are often preferred in fixed locations for reliability and ease of integration with existing systems.</p></p>
