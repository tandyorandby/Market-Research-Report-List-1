<p><p>The watch cleaning machines market features two primary types: steam and ultrasonic. Steam cleaning machines utilize high-pressure steam to effectively remove dirt and grime, ensuring a deep clean without harmful chemicals. In contrast, ultrasonic cleaning machines employ high-frequency sound waves in a liquid solution, creating tiny bubbles that gently cleanse intricate watch components. Both types are essential for maintaining watch hygiene and functionality, catering to various user preferences in professional watch repairs and personal maintenance.</p></p>
