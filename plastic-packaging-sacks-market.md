<p><p>The plastic packaging sacks market encompasses various types, each serving distinct purposes. PE (Polyethylene) plastic is widely used for its flexibility and durability, suitable for various applications. PVA (Polyvinyl Alcohol) plastic is biodegradable, often used for environmentally-friendly packaging solutions.</p></p>
