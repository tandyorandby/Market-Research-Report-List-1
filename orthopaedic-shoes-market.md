<p><p>The orthopaedic shoes market is categorized into several types based on specific foot conditions. Flatfoot orthopaedic shoes provide support for individuals with flat arches, maintaining alignment and comfort. Cavus orthopaedic shoes cater to those with high arches, offering cushioning and stability. Calcaneal spur orthopaedic shoes are designed for heel spur sufferers, alleviating pain through specialized support. Varus orthopaedic shoes correct inward foot positioning, while valgus orthopaedic shoes address outward positioning. Additionally, "others" include shoes for various other foot ailments and conditions.</p></p>
