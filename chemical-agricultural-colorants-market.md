<p><p>The Chemical Agricultural Colorants Market is segmented into two main types: dyes and pigments. Dyes are water-soluble colorants that provide vibrant hues and are often used in applications like seed coating and plant treatments to enhance visual appeal and identify crop types. Pigments, in contrast, are insoluble colorants that offer durability and resistance to light and weather conditions, making them ideal for use in fertilizers and other solid applications. Both types contribute to improved aesthetic value and functionality in agriculture.</p></p>
