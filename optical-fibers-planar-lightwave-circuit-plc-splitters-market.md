<p><p>Optical Fibers Planar Lightwave Circuit (PLC) Splitters are essential components in fiber optic networks, facilitating signal distribution. The 1×N PLC splitter divides a single input signal into multiple output signals, making it ideal for applications where one source feeds several destinations. Conversely, the 2×N PLC splitter amplifies flexibility by allowing two input signals to be split among N outputs, catering to more complex networks. Both types enhance scalability and efficiency in telecommunications and data transmission systems.</p></p>
