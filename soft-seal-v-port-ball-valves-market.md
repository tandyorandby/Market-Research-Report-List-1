<p><p>Soft seal V port ball valves are essential components in fluid control systems, available in two primary types: flangedless and flanged. Flangedless valves feature a compact design for easy integration into existing pipelines without the need for flanges, offering versatility and reduced installation costs. In contrast, flanged valves come with pre-attached flanges for a secure, robust connection, suitable for larger or high-pressure applications. Both types utilize a V-shaped port design for precise flow regulation, enhancing operational efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
