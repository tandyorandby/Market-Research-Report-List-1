<p><p>The Groove Barcode Reading Device market is divided into two main types: Visible Light and Infrared. Visible Light scanners utilize standard light wavelengths to capture and decode barcode information, offering high accuracy and speed in well-lit conditions. In contrast, Infrared scanners operate by detecting infrared light, allowing for reading in low-light or challenging environments. Each type caters to specific applications, with visible light systems suited for general retail and infrared options preferred in specialized settings where lighting is inconsistent.</p></p>
