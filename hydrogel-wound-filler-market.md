<p><p>The hydrogel wound filler market is primarily categorized into two types: gel and spray formats. Gel-based hydrogels are thick, viscous materials that provide a moisture-rich environment for wound healing, making them ideal for deeper or larger wounds. On the other hand, spray hydrogel offers a convenient application method, delivering a fine mist that covers wounds with a protective layer, suitable for superficial injuries. Both types facilitate healing by maintaining moisture, reducing pain, and preventing infections.</p></p>
