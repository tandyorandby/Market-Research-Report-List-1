<p><p>The Short Glass Fiber Thermoplastic market is primarily categorized into two processing methods: injection molding and extrusion molding. Injection molding involves injecting molten thermoplastic mixed with short glass fibers into a mold, allowing for intricate shapes and high production rates. In contrast, extrusion molding involves forcing the thermoplastic through a die, shaping it into continuous profiles or sheets. Both methods enhance the mechanical properties of the thermoplastic, making them ideal for lightweight yet strong applications in automotive, aerospace, and consumer goods industries.</p></p>
