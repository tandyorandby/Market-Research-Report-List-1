<p><p>Indoor Positioning Service (IPS) and Outdoor Positioning Service (OPS) refer to technologies that enable precise location tracking in diverse environments. IPS focuses on identifying user locations within buildings, leveraging systems like Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and RFID for improved navigation in complex infrastructures like malls and airports. In contrast, OPS utilizes global navigation satellite systems (GNSS) and other technologies to provide accurate positioning outdoors, essential for applications like route navigation, fleet management, and location-based services. Together, they enhance user experience across various sectors.</p></p>
