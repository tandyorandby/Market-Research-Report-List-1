<p><strong>The Clot Management Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inferior Vena Cava Filters(IVCF)</li><li>Neurovascular Embolectomy Devices</li><li>Catheter-Directed Thrombolysis(CDT) Devices</li><li>Percutaneous Thrombectomy Devices</li><li>Embolectomy Balloon Catheters/Clot Management Catheter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Clot Management Device Market encompasses various types designed to treat vascular clots. Inferior Vena Cava Filters (IVCF) are used to prevent blood clots from reaching the lungs. Neurovascular Embolectomy Devices focus on removing clots in cerebral arteries. Catheter-Directed Thrombolysis (CDT) Devices deliver medication directly to dissolve clots. Percutaneous Thrombectomy Devices mechanically remove clots from blood vessels, while Embolectomy Balloon Catheters/Clot Management Catheters aid in retrieval and aspiration of clots, enhancing overall vascular health outcomes.</p></p>
