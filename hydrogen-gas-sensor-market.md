<p><p>The hydrogen gas sensor market is divided into several types: </p><p>1. **Electrochemical Sensors**: These sensors measure hydrogen concentration by detecting the current produced during a chemical reaction. </p><p>2. **Metal Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) Sensors**: They use metal oxide materials to detect hydrogen through changes in resistance when exposed to gas. </p><p>3. **Catalytic Sensors**: These sensors rely on a catalytic reaction that generates heat in the presence of hydrogen, indicating its concentration. </p><p>4. **Others**: This category includes emerging technologies or specialized sensors not fitting into the above classifications.</p></p>
