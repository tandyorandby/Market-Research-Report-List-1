<p><strong>The ArF Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ArF Immersion Scanner</li><li>Dry ArF Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ArF scanner market is primarily divided into two types: ArF immersion scanners and dry ArF scanners. ArF immersion scanners utilize a layer of liquid between the lens and the wafer, enhancing resolution and enabling finer patterns crucial for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, dry ArF scanners operate in air, using traditional optics, making them suitable for less complex processes. Both types play integral roles in photolithography, catering to different technology nodes and production requirements in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
