<p><strong>The Electric Power Steering Control System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Hydraulic</li><li>Electrohydraulic</li><li>Electric Assist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Power Steering (EPS) control system market is divided into three main types: Mechanical Hydraulic, Electrohydraulic, and Electric Assist. Mechanical Hydraulic systems rely on hydraulic pumps driven by the engine, offering good maneuverability but less efficiency. Electrohydraulic systems integrate electric motor assistance with hydraulic components, balancing responsiveness and energy efficiency. Electric Assist systems use electric motors solely to provide steering support, enhancing fuel efficiency and responsiveness while reducing weight and mechanical complexity, making them increasingly popular in modern vehicles.</p></p>
