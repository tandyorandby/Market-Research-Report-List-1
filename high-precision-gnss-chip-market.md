<p><ul><li>Dual Receiver</li><li>Multiple Receiver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Precision GNSS Chip market includes two primary types: dual receiver and multiple receiver systems. Dual receiver systems utilize two GNSS signals for enhanced accuracy and redundancy, making them suitable for applications requiring precision like surveying and autonomous vehicles. Multiple receiver systems integrate signals from several GNSS sources, significantly boosting positioning accuracy and robustness. This type is ideal for environments with obstructed signals, such as urban canyons, where precise navigation is crucial for various industries, including aviation and agriculture.</p></p>
